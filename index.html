PROJECT OVERVIEW

The Challenge

Our development team currently faces several critical bottlenecks:

  • No visibility into developer capacity when briefing new work
  • Over-commitment leading to missed deadlines
  • Manual scheduling causing delays and miscommunication
  • Difficulty tracking actual vs estimated hours
  • Clients receiving unrealistic timeline expectations
  • Time tracking disconnected from project planning

The Solution

An intelligent capacity management system that:

  • Shows real-time developer availability
  • Auto-schedules tasks based on capacity and priority
  • Integrates Monday.com, 7pace & Google Calendar
  • Provides accurate timeline predictions
  • Prevents over-booking with smart allocation
  • Tracks performance for continuous improvement
